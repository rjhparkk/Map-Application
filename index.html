Niagara Winery Finder

About This Website

This website is designed for tourists and locals who want to discover wineries in the Niagara-on-the-Lake area. On this site, you can view a map with markers for various wineries, filter them by category (e.g., Organic, With Tours, Restaurant), and even add new wineries if you know one.

How to use:

  • Use the filter buttons to display only wineries of a specific category.
  • Click on a winery marker to view its details and access a "Get Directions" feature.
  • Click "Show My Location" to display your current location on the map.
  • Use the provided form to add a new winery by entering its name, address, and selecting a category.
